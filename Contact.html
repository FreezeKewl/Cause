The Earth is a closed system, similar to a terrarium, meaning that it rarely loses or gains extra matter. The same water that existed on the earth millions of years ago is still present today. Somewhere between 70 and 75 percent of the earth’s surface is covered with water. Similarly, 70 percent of an adult’s body is made up of water.
